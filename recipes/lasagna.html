Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish which consists of layers of pasta, tomato meat sauce and white cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the pasta
  3. Make cheese mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let it rest
